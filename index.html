The meaning of Alphaura and its signature

Alphaura

In reality the word "Alphaura" is actualy 2 words, which are "Alpha" and "Aura". The wording of the brand name is mainly referring to two things;

Alpha; The alpha wolf concept of fighting for what you want, the alpha wave and the power of mindfulness and the flow

Aura; The wolf's spirit meaning -> Strong connection with your instincts or intuition, High intelligence, Loyalty and communication, Deep desire for freedom

These are the foundations the name brand is built on. As a person, I've always looked for the next great thing, yet, I've tried too hard, without any doing. Once I understood the myself, my journey, and the meaning and power of mindfulness, I started doing, hence why, I created this brand and its brand name to share this knowledge in a creative way.

Signature

I wanted to create something extra, were one can understand that this is Alphaura, without needing to read the actual brand name, so I created a signature. I wasn't sure what to do as a signature, but one night while I was setting of to sleep, the "Alpha" wave came in and did its thing. The signature is also split in two parts, one being the "A" for Alphaura, and the biggest part is the actual "Alpha Wave".
